Macaroni & Cheese

Description

How to create my award winning Macaroni & Cheese. Created in a small town restaurant in Michigan, I believe this is the only recipe you'll ever need when it comes to homemade Macaroni & Cheese. Keep in mind this recipe contains no protiens or noticable vegetables so feel free to add whatever you would like! :)


Ingredients

  • American Cheese, Smoked Gouda, Cheddar and Jarlsburg
  • 1/2 LB Butter
  • 1 Sweet Onion
  • 6 Cloves Fresh Garlic
  • 1QT Heavy Cream
  • 1c Milk
  • 1/4c White Wine
  • 1/2c flour
  • 1 Box Macaroni or 1 LB Homemade Pasta

Steps

  1. Melt your butter in a large sauce pot and add your finely chopped onion, cooking on low to medium heat until translucent.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, constantly stirring. Add white wine to stop the garlic from cooking and deglaze the pot.
  3. Add flour and stir until flour and butter is mixed and smooth.
  4. Add heavy cream and milk slowly while constantly stirring, be sure to not scrape the bottom of the pot.
  5. Slowly add cheese, stir until all cheese is melted.
  6. Add your pasta to your cheese sauce, toss and enjoy!
