Air Fryer Brussels Sprouts

Description

A quick 10 minute recipe for Air Fryer Brussels Sprouts including bacon and balsamic reduction!

Ingredients

  • 1t Cooking Oil
  • 1/2tsp Salt
  • 1/2tsp Black Pepper
  • 1lb Fresh Brussels Sprouts
  • 1Tb Balsamic Reduction
  • 2Tb Crumbled Cooked Bacon

Directions

  1. Trim the end of your Brussels Sprouts and half them, combine in bowl with oil, salt and pepper.
  2. Air Fry for 5-6 minutes at 350 degrees.
  3. Toss with cooked bacon and balsamic reduction.
